The approach

How I work

Agentic engineering isn't a side tool for me — it's the primary workflow. Most routine engineering is delegated to loops I built and tuned; I work as the architect and the steering hand.

I lead AI product engineering at YTL AI Labs, where I've worked on ILMUchat — Malaysia's public conversational-AI product. I now focus on experimental, highly sensitive work, and I set the engineering practices that let a small team ship fast without chaos.

Underneath that is a habit: research-first, TDD-guided, review-immediately. New code gets a review agent the moment it's written. Long jobs run detached and notify on state change. Reasoning runs on the strong model; high-volume review and extraction fan out to a cheaper one — cost-aware by default.

Design rule Every mechanism must be project-agnostic. Build it once; express per-repo specifics as declarative config a repo adopts by adding a file.
